Cue Spaces

🔔 spaces — early on the bell predicts a bonus. Later it becomes unreliable, then stops entirely.

The Three Conditions

Condition 1
Acquisition (Spaces 1–7) — delayed reward pays off, 🔔 cue always gives a bonus.
Condition 2
Increased Delay (Spaces 8–14) — delayed reward takes longer, 🔔 cue is only sometimes reliable.
Condition 3
Extinction (Spaces 15–21) — delayed reward stops paying off, 🔔 cue gives no bonus.

What we think will happen

  • Players will choose the delayed reward more when the wait is short.
  • Players will shift toward immediate rewards when the delay gets longer.
  • Players may keep trying the delayed option even after it stops working.
  • The 🔔 cue may still affect behavior after it stops predicting reward.
  • Wrong answers cause lost moves, creating a real cost for incorrect responding.
  • Reaching the Finish requires a correct Final Challenge answer — knowledge wins!
